Who am I?

I am a boat dwelling truck driver, doing a degree in Computing and IT with the Open University. I am in my 4th year, which is the latter part of the second year of a full time degree. I'm on for a first class degree with distinction, which is taking a lot of work. I work 50+ hours a week as a truck driver for DHL on an M&S contract. Alas, our depot is about to get shut down and I really need to take this opportunity to move over into my chosen profession of being a software developer a little earlier than I had anticipated.

I am incredibly driven and motivated, and a very fast learner. I built this website from a bootstrap template in a weekend, having never done anything of the sort in my life, this is my first website. It has even got a contacts section that does input into my MySQL database, if only currently locally (see Databases)

I am outgoing and have a great sense of humour. I also play piano, flute and sing for fun, when I get the chance. I live aboard my narrowboat with my cat, and Kermit my trucking buddy comes around the country with me in the truck, and sometimes to band practice.
